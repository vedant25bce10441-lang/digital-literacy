Project Report

For this task, I used Canva as my design tool to create a single infographic. Canva is a free and user-friendly platform that offers a variety of templates, icons, and design elements, which made the process smooth and creative.

My infographic covers three main topics: digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, and safe internet practices. In the digital literacy section, I explained how it involves the ability to find, evaluate, and use information online effectively. For useful digital tools, I included examples like Google Docs, Zoom, and educational apps that help students collaborate and learn efficiently. In the safe internet practices section, I highlighted tips such as using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal information.

One interesting aspect of this project was experimenting with different layouts and color combinations to make the infographic visually appealing while still easy to understand. However, I found it slightly challenging to balance the amount of information and design elements without making the slide look too crowded.

Overall, this task helped me improve both my digital design skills and understanding of important online practices.
