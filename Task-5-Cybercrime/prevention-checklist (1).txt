Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
- Always verify UPI collect requests before approving; receiving money does NOT require approval.
- Avoid clicking on unknown links received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
- Install apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
- Regularly update your phone and apps to protect against security vulnerabilities.
- Check website URLs carefully; ensure they start with HTTPS before entering sensitive information.
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions like UPI payments or online banking.
- Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public computers.
- Be cautious of job, internship, or prize scams asking for upfront payments.

Reporting Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
