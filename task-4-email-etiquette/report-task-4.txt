Project Report

One situation where poor digital communication caused a problem is when a student sent an email to a professor using informal language and unclear wording. The message lacked a proper subject line and did not clearly explain the issue, which led to confusion and a delayed response. As a result, the student missed an important deadline.

This situation highlights the importance of clear and professional communication in digital platforms. The student could have written a proper subject line, used polite language, and explained the problem in a structured way. Proofreading the message before sending would also have helped avoid misunderstandings.

Effective digital communication ensures that messages are understood correctly and helps maintain a positive impression. It is especially important in academic and professional environments where clarity and professionalism are expected.
