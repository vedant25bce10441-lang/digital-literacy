Social Media Responsibility Checklist

Do's
- Think before you post; ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Maintain a professional tone, especially when interacting with faculty or organizations.
- Use social media to build a positive digital footprint (achievements, skills, learning).

Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords).
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
- Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts.
- Do not blindly trust or share unverified news or links.
